COMPANY

LLP «West Development Company» was founded in 2013 and today is one of the main companies in the Republic of Kazakhstan, performing a wide range of construction and installation works at oil refinery, gas processing plant and petrochemical facilities. As a result of successful activities, the company has established itself as a reliable contractor and took part in the development of landmark projects in the territory of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Thanks to the highly qualified engineering staff, West Development Company implements projects of increased complexity, where a high level of quality, safety and management is required.

► SANDBLASTING AND PREVENTIVE COATING APPLICATION
    Sandblasting of metal is a process of cleaning the surfaces of metal structures and other metal products from traces of corrosion, soot, old coatings (varnishes, paints), scale after welding or cutting, foreign deposits by exposing them to a mixture of air with particles of abrasive materials supplied through a nozzle under high pressure to the place of metalworking.
   West Development Company is a certified for the application of basic passive fire protection systems (thin-layer thermal expansion joints). Such systems have a fire resistance of up to 120 minutes.
